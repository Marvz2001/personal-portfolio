EXPLANATION:

Love does not often works as we would like too. We cannot chose who we love or who loves us, yet love is vital for our well-being. 
We feel alive when we love, and we feel cherished, appreciated, and cared for when we are loved. A true love, however, can only be mutual. 
A requited love is the only type of love that fulfils its purpose completely. When there is mutual attraction, and emotional, intellectual, 
and spiritual connection, love becomes invincible.
