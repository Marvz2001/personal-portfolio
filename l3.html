EXPLANATION:

Because it is so emotionally intense, love changes our entire outlook on life. When we are in love, everything seems radiant around us. 
Love puts rose-colored glasses on our eyes and we became romantic and idealistic. Love touches the deepest parts of our soul and makes us see 
the world through the lens of our feelings, exactly what poets do. Our only chance to see the world as rich as beautiful as poets do is to remain forever in love.
