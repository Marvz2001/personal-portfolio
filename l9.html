EXPLANATION:

In its beginnings, a love relationship is more about passion than commitment, that is why it appears as more fragile and easier to break.
However, with time comes a lot of shared experience, with hundreds of moments of pure connection, happiness, and support in times of struggle.
As the number of these moments increases, love becomes stronger. Love is like a construction on which we have to work constantly, and only time can 
give it its proper shape.
